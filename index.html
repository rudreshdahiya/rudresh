about me

Rudresh

Hi, I am Rudresh. An MBA graduate from SPJIMR. My academics gave me a strong foundation in global business strategy. I apply this framework to solve problems in a practical, hands-on way.

My unique strength is in translating strategy into execution. For example, I built a consumer-facing e-commerce brand from the ground up. This meant identifying the market need, developing the product, managing the supply chain, and leading a go-to-market strategy that resulted in a profitable venture. It was a complete business lifecycle, from concept to revenue.

This same blend of analytical and practical skills has been valuable in consulting projects for organizations like the World Bank and IDH, where I developed data products to improve transparency and measure effectiveness. My background has trained me to navigate ambiguity and connect with stakeholders at all levels, from end-users to leadership, to find the core of a problem and build a solution that works.

I am confident in my ability to bring this adaptable, results-oriented mindset to a corporate environment and deliver value from day one.
